OPEN ROLES

Current Openings

We're growing fast and looking for talented people to join our team.

Freight Broker / Carrier Sales Representative

Michigan Full-Time On-Site

Build and manage carrier relationships across our nationwide network. Negotiate competitive rates, book loads, and ensure capacity for our clients. Ideal for self-starters with strong communication skills and a drive to build a book of business.

Logistics Coordinator

Michigan Full-Time On-Site

Coordinate day-to-day shipments from pickup to delivery. Track loads in real time, communicate with drivers and shippers, resolve issues proactively, and ensure every load delivers on time. Great entry point into the logistics industry.

Operations Manager

Michigan Full-Time Management

Oversee daily logistics operations, lead a team of coordinators and dispatchers, optimize processes, and drive operational KPIs. 3+ years of logistics management experience required. This role reports directly to senior leadership.
