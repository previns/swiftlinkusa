About SwiftLink Logistics

Your trusted partner for freight transportation across the continental United States.

Our Story

Built on the Principle That Every Shipment Matters

SwiftLink Logistics, LLC is a third-party logistics (3PL) provider headquartered in Michigan. Founded on the principle that every shipment matters, we connect shippers with a vetted network of 5,000+ carrier partners across all 48 contiguous states.

Our team combines deep industry expertise with technology-driven solutions to deliver reliable, transparent freight management. From a single pallet to a full truckload, we treat every load with the same level of care and urgency.

Our Mission

To simplify freight transportation by providing shippers with reliable capacity, real-time visibility, and dedicated support — at competitive rates. We believe logistics should be a competitive advantage, not a headache.
